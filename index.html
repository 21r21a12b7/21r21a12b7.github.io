ABOUT-APP

Where Is My Bus is a mobile application designed to provide real - time
information about the location and status of buses in a transportation
system. The primary goal of such an app is to enhance the overall
commuting experience for users by offering them accurate and up-to-
date information about bus arrivals, departures, and current locations
