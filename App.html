1.LOGIN

This is the login page where you will login with your phone number and password and even if you forget your password no problem you create new by clicking forget password,when you entered both number and password and click on login you are redirected to home page.

2.LOCATION ACCESS:

App will ask your permission to give access that will access the current location of your mobile phone to track your live locations.

3.TRAVEL DETAILS:

Select the travel details that is home to collage || collage to home || you can also search by bus by clicking SEARCH BY BUS button.

4.AVAILABLE BUS ROUTES:

Here you will see the available bus routes and select your bus route and click on GO button.

5.LOCATION OF BUS:

When you click on go button after selecting bus route you will be directed to page where you can see the live location of your bus,this is the page where you can see your bus location and also you can come to know arriving time of the bus.

6.MORE OPTIONS:

When you click on more options you can see many options like TIME TABLE | STOPS | BUS MAPS | SUPPORT | REPORT ISSUE and also you can change language.

7.BUS STOPS:

When you click on bus stops in more options then you will be directed to this page, In this page you will see all the stops from bus route starting to ending and also stops which are bus is visited will be marked in yellow colour and not visited stops will be marked in purple colour by this you will get to know at which stop currently the bus is.
